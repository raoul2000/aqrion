The Company

AQrion Medical is a Swiss company that commercialize a state of the art navigation system enabling faster and more accurate placement of pedicle screws during spinal fusion surgeries

The result of a joint program between Universitatspital Basel and Universitat Basel Department of Biomedical Engineering, AQrion Medical was brought on to fully develop the existing prototypes and commercialize the project.

The company primary focus is on robot-assisted surgery for spinal surgery through the commercialization of the NaviPenTM, a robotic platform system that brings the advantages to patients while enabling surgeons with innovative technology .
